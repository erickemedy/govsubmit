CISA Cybersecurity Report

Report hacking, ransomware, or other cyber incidents

CISA Cybersecurity Incident Report

Report hacking, ransomware, data breaches, or other cyber incidents (not robocalls).

You will review and submit your incident report directly on CISA’s official site.

For potential crimes, report to the FBI Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3). Serious threats may also be reported to your local state fusion center. These are separate official channels.
